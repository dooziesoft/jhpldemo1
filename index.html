About Jay Shree Horologicals Pvt Ltd

Jayashree Horological, Established in the year 1997 is a Bengaluru based firm that is engaged in manufacturing and supplying a range of superior quality plastic watch case, industrial precision tools, gasket watch case, pinion odometer, hydraulic pump parts, and others. Mentor of the firm Mr. Lakshmipathy K. has always worked as a constant guide for the team and helped it to set goals and meet firm targets. His vision has helped us to be ahead of the competitors and keep going in the industrial frontiers at a fast pace.

Our products are manufactured using high grade raw materials and engineered with precision following engineering models drawn by engineering professionals. While engineering these products, we do pay attention on varied parameters and follow international industrial standards to ensure that our product complies well to industrial needs.

Supported by a team of designers, mechanical engineers, technicians and quality analysts, we have been able to maintain a professional environment within the firm. The team functional with us has a through knowledge of industrial demands and utilizes the technical facilities available with us with great competence to ensure flawless and matchless products are offered by us.

Our industrial norms and business policies are devised to ensure satisfaction of clients by complementing well to their technical needs and requirements. Easy payment modes, instant feedback to client queries, on-site technical support and other facilities offered by us makes us one of the most trusted partners of clients.
